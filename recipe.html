Chocolate Lava Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 4 oz dark chocolate
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 425¬∞F (220¬∞C) and grease ramekins.
  2. Melt chocolate and butter together, mix until smooth.
  3. In a bowl, whisk eggs, egg yolks, and sugar until thick.
  4. Fold in melted chocolate mixture and add flour and salt.
  5. Pour batter into ramekins and bake for 12-14 minutes.
  6. Let it rest for a minute, then serve warm with ice cream.
